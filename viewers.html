viewers analysis

joshua daniel ascuncion

People stream movies and tv shows because of the flexibility of the amount of content they can watch and for the ability to watch older titles fulfilling a want to feel nostalgia.
People stream podcasts to listen to their favorite celebrities talk about a certain topics for a few hours.
People stream music videos to uplift their mood.
People watch vlogs to see what their favorite vloggers do in their daily life.
People watch gaming streams for a lot of reasons, one of which is the social aspect of interacting with your favorite streamers. Just even hearing a big celebrity with millions of followers call you by name is enough to make a grown man cry out in excitement. And of course, to get better at the game they are watching.
